About the Alliance

Daedeok Global TLO Alliance (DGTA) is Korea's official gateway for deep-tech technology transfer. Under the leadership of the Ministry of Science and ICT, DGTA unites the collective research excellence of over 30 leading research institutes and universities in Daedeok Innopolis into a single, cohesive global network.


Our mission is to provide a seamless, 'one-stop' platform that connects international partners with verified Korean deep technologies and ready-to-scale spin-offs. DGTA streamlines the process of global matching, licensing, and joint R&BD, creating sustainable innovation bridges with global clusters like Quebec and Grenoble.
